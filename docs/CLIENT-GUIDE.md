# Client user guide

This guide is for customers who browse and order embroidery designs.

## Creating an account

1. Open `https://your-site.com/register`.
2. Fill in your name, email, and a password (8+ characters). Phone, company, and address are optional but help if you'll request custom work.
3. Click **Create account** — you're now signed in.

## Browsing designs

- The **Gallery** page lists every published design with thumbnail, category, and price.
- Use the **search** box to filter by title, description, or tag.
- Use the **category chips** to narrow to e.g. floral, geometric, lettering.
- Click any design card to see full details and the original file preview.

## Downloading a design

- On the design detail page, click **Download file**.
- You'll need to be signed in to download. Guests are redirected to login.
- Each download is logged (visible to admin only).

## Messaging the business

- Open **My messages** in the sidebar.
- Compose a new thread with a subject and your question.
- Replies from the business appear in the same thread. You'll see the status badge:
  - **open** — waiting for the business
  - **replied** — the business has answered
  - **closed** — the business marked the thread as resolved
- You can reply to keep the thread going; this reopens it.

## Updating your profile

- Open **My profile** in the sidebar.
- Edit your name, phone, company, or address and click **Save profile**.
- Use **Change password** to set a new password (you'll need to enter your current one first).

## Account disabled?

If you try to sign in and see "Account disabled", the business owner has temporarily blocked your account. Reach out via the contact form on the public site to resolve it.
